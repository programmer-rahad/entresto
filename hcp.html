ENTRESTO® (sacubitril/valsartan) is indicated for the treatment of heart failure with reduced ejection fraction (HFrEF) in patients with NYHA Class II or III, to reduce the incidence of cardiovascular (CV) death and heart failure (HF) hospitalization.

ENTRESTO® should be administered in combination with other heart failure therapies, in place of an angiotensin-converting enzyme inhibitor (ACEi) or angiotensin II receptor blocker (ARB).

20% reduced risk of composite CV death or first HF hospitalization‡ was demonstrated with ENTRESTO® vs. enalapril
